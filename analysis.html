Final Insights

Spotify success is not explained by one feature alone. The highest-streamed songs usually combine broad appeal, artist recognition, replay value, playlist exposure, and audio traits that fit listener preferences. Based on this dataset, platform visibility and overall popularity signals appear more useful than relying only on danceability, energy, or mode.
